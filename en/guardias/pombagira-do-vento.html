Pombagira do Vento

“I take with the wind what no longer serves you.”

Leveza, Mensagens Rápidas e Dissipação de Energias. Trabalha com o elemento ar, com os ventos que trazem mudanças. Rege as mensagens rápidas, a comunicação, a dissipação de energias densas e a leveza espiritual. É invocada para quebrar demandas de vento (inveja falada), agilizar processos e trazer notícias.

Sacred Attributes & Elements

Offering Light lavender or lavender incense burned in the wind, sweet apple champagne, very light white and blue ribbons
Colors Wind white, Sky blue, Cloud gray, Air silver, Light yellow
Candles White, Light blue, Silver (light candles that burn quickly, stick or small)
Place of Strength Hill with strong wind, cliff edge, open field, high roof, drafty spot
Ideal Time 3pm (afternoon wind), Tuesday at sunrise, Saturday at 5pm
Ideal Moon Crescent Moon (wind that brings news) or New Moon (renewing wind)
Regency / Affinity Movement / Iansã
Day of Strength Sunday

History & Mysteries

The mystery of Pombagira do Vento represents one of the most respected phalanxes of Afro-Brazilian religions. Far from distorted legends created by Western prejudice, this guardian acts as a true lighthouse in the shadows, protecting free will, reestablishing karmic justice and supporting those who go through moments of deep anguish.

Every detail of your visual and energetic presentation — from the nuances of your favorite colors to the exact place where you receive offerings — carries a deep spiritual coding. Her stories tell journeys of overcoming, empowerment and transmutation of pain into wisdom, teaching that the true strength of the sacred feminine lies in dignity and loyalty to one's own principles.

Chanted Point of Strength:

A ela vem girando e cantando com fé!
É Pombagira do Vento que reina no terreiro,
Trazendo sua luz para iluminar quem quer.”

Foundations & Field of Action

In the cosmology of Umbanda and Quimbanda, Pombagira do Vento acts directly on the vibration of Movement / Iansã. She masters the manipulation of vital and emotional energies, being an expert in undoing heavy negative magic, dissolving blockages that impede the flow of prosperity and reorganizing the affective field of her proteges.

She teaches that attraction and abundance in life are the result of constant movement and the courage to break new ground. Under the influence of your ideal moon (Crescent Moon (wind that brings news) or New Moon (renewing wind)), your energy becomes a powerful catalyst for opening paths, ensuring that no obstacle can suffocate the journey of those who knock on your door with sincerity and real faith.

Prayer and Consecration to Pombagira do Vento

“Hail your strength, sovereign Guardian Pombagira of the Wind! To you, lady of the paths and holder of the mystery of fire and wind, I ask for your protection. Cut with your strength and determination the visible and invisible ties that try to bind me. Dispel the fog of lies and betrayal that surround my steps. Teach me to rise with your royalty, to walk with your sovereign elegance and to reign over my own desires. May on the ideal moon of your mystery, my life flourish in axé, love, abundance and constant protection. Laroyé, Guardian of Light! Saravá your mystery!”
